Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set a medicine reminder? add
You can go to the Reminders section, click "Add Reminder", and choose time, date, and medicine name.
Can I change the exercise duration? add
Yes, in the Exercise section you can set your preferred timer before starting.
What is street view for? add
Tap on the Street View feature, search for any location like your hometown or your family's address, and explore it using Google Maps.
How does voice navigation work? add
Voice navigation uses Google Speech API. Tap the microphone icon and speak your command like “Open Games” or “Show Diet Plans”. The app will respond and switch tabs for you.
Is there a diet plan for specific health needs? add
Yes. We provide tailored diet plans for diabetes, heart health, bone strength, and energy. Go to the Diet Plans section and choose what suits you.
How do I activate the magnifier bubble? add
You can double-tap anywhere on the screen to activate or deactivate the magnifier bubble. It helps enlarge text for better readability.
Where can I find exercise videos? add
In the Exercise section, you'll find embedded YouTube videos curated for seniors. Tap on a video to start your workout along with a built-in timer.
